What's in My Drink?

Project Info:

"What's in my Drink?" is a website that provides users with an easy-to-navigate encyclopedia of cocktails. The website allows users to search for specific drinks, browse drinks by category, get 10 random drinks at a time, or view the most popular drinks. Each drink card includes a picture of the finished product and the name of the drink. When a user clicks on a drink, they can view detailed information about the drink, including its ingredients, instructions on how to make it, and nutritional information. This website is geared towards people who enjoy mixing and trying different cocktails. It offers a comprehensive resource for those who are looking to discover new drinks or want to know more about the ingredients and nutritional content of their favorite cocktails.

During the project, I was the primary developer of the search by name feature, which allows users to search for cocktails using keywords in their name. I also developed the Top Ten drinks page, which displays the most popular drinks in the API. These features were implemented using a range of programming languages and technologies, and required a thorough understanding of the project's requirements and goals. I also contributed to the testing and debugging of these features to ensure that they were functioning correctly and providing a positive user experience.
